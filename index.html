    Why This Works

    Foundations

    • Faithfulness in small details compounds over time
    • First fruits anchor the day in Christ before everything else
    • Steward talents for neighbor and kingdom
    • Measure to improve — data, not shame
    • Non-negotiables with honest carry-over beat vague goals

    Scriptures

    "Whatever you do, do it from the heart, as something done for the Lord and not for people." — Colossians 3:23

    "So, whether you eat or drink, or whatever you do, do everything for the glory of God." — 1 Corinthians 10:31

    "Well done, good and faithful servant… You were faithful over a few things; I will put you in charge of many things." — Matthew 25:21

    "Commit your activities to the LORD, and your plans will be established." — Proverbs 16:3
